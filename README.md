### Inflation dataset information:

1. B30 – 30 Year Bond
Interest rate / yield on a 30-year US Treasury bond
Used to track long-term interest rates

2. B20 – 20 Year Bond
Yield on a 20-year Treasury bond
Long-term, but slightly shorter than 30-year

3. B10 – 10 Year Bond
Yield on a 10-year Treasury bond
Very important benchmark for the economy and markets

4. B7 – 7 Year Bond
Yield on a 7-year Treasury bond
Medium-term government borrowing rate

5. B5 – 5 Year Bond
Yield on a 5-year Treasury bond
Often used for medium-term investment analysis

6. B2 – 2 Year Bond
Yield on a 2-year Treasury bond
Closely linked to short-term interest rate expectations

7. B1 – 1 Year Bond
Yield on a 1-year Treasury bond
Short-term government borrowing rate

8. T90 – 90 Day Bill
Interest rate on a 3-month (90-day) Treasury bill
Used as a short-term risk-free rate

9. T30 – 30 Day Bill Interest rate on a1-month (30-day) Treasury bill
Very short-term interest rate

10. CPI – Consumer Price Index
